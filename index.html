About Us

Sai's Orphanage is a Non-Governmental Organisation(NGO) that supports underprivileged children in India. We collectively help the children in need. We rescue children from child labor, sexual abuse, violence, child trafficking, addiction, runaways, severe health conditions, child marriage, and educational purposes, etc. We assure the brighter future of our kids and ensure that they reach great heights in their careers. NGOs, sometimes called civil societies, are organized on community, national and international levels to serve a social or political goal such as humanitarian causes or the environment.
